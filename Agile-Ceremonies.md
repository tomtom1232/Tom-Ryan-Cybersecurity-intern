What are the main Agile ceremonies, and what is their purpose?
o	Daily Stand-ups, these are usually 5-15 minutes every weekday. What will you work on next, and what will I work on for the rest of the day, any blockers that I have as well. 
o	Sprint Planning (Scrum) vs. Continuous Prioritization (Kanban),This meeting happens at the start of the sprint, where the team selects the tasks from the backlog and plans. 
o	Retrospectives, this meeting is held when the team is at the end of a sprint and will reflect on what went well and what didn’t go to plan during the sprint and what they can do differently next sprint. 
o	Backlog Refinement, A session where the team reviews backlog items, sees requirement and establishes effort. 
How do Agile teams collaborate asynchronously and across time zones? 
Agile teams working in different time zones use tools like Microsoft Teams and Jira. 
These platforms can communicate without updates needed everyone is online at once, you can work at your own pace anytime, anywhere.
