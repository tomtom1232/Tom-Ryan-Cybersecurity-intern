Why do you think Focus Bear was created?
Focus Bear was created to help people with ADHD focus on completing their tasks without distractions. 
There are also features for people to do meditation and be active and not be on a computer or screen for excessive hours per day.  

What problem is Focus Bear solving? 
It solves the problem many people face, which is distraction from technology, such as phones or computers. 
It blocks distractions on your phone and or computer to help you focus on getting your required tasks done.

Why do you think this mission is important?
Yes, I believe this mission is very important as ADHD diagnoses are rising there needs to be a solution, and focus bear could be an improvement in many people with ADHD’s lives.


How does Focus Bear’s work align with your personal values or interests?
Focus Bear does align with my personal values and interests, as I can find it hard to focus and can be distracted. 
The goal setting and meditation section of the app are things that I will try and am interested in. 


Do you personally relate to any of the challenges that Focus Bear aims to solve?
Personally, I don’t have ADHD however, I do find it challenging to focus on work sometimes. 
Phones can be very distracting, I can admit to that, with getting caught up in screen time and social media. 
So now having Focus Bear on my phone, I have noticed slight improvements in my concentration with university work.
