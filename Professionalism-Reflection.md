🔍 Research & Learn
What are examples of professional vs. unprofessional behaviour in a casual work environment? (Hint: Think about communication, meeting etiquette, and teamwork.)
Professional, communicating respectfully while being reliable and contributing to the team in a positive manner. This can include responding to an email clearly and being prepared for stand ups and meetings. 
Unprofessional, this may include ignoring emails and or messages, being rude to any team member or missing deadlines without communication, also making others feel uncomfortable. 

What does respectful communication look like in remote teams? (e.g., tone in messages, handling disagreements, responding to feedback)
Some respectful communication can be writing messages with clear and police tone. We as team members should avoid aggressive language and should focus on solving problems together respectfully. 

How can you give and receive feedback professionally? (e.g., focus on actions, not people; assume good intent; ask for clarification instead of reacting defensively)
When giving feedback it is vital to focus on the work rather than the person. Feedback should be constructive and clear to aim at improving. 
When receiving feedback, it helps for me to stay open minded and avoid reacting defensively. To listening carefully and ask clarifying questions and viewing feedback as an opportunity to grow and improve on my skills.
