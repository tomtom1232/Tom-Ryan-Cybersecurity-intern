What are the risks of using a laptop without an external monitor or keyboard?
It can be bad for posture, and you could be looking down hurting your neck or hunched forward. This could lead to injures, it can also lead to strain injuries in the wrist and hands. 
What ergonomic equipment can improve posture when working on a laptop? (e.g., laptop stands, external keyboards, ergonomic chairs) 
I already have an ergonomic desk chair this helps my posture. Another item could be a laptop stand and an external keyboard. 

What adjustments should be made to monitor height, chair position, and desk setup for a healthier workspace?
The monitor level should be at eye level, and the screen should be an arm’s length away. The chair should support the lower back, and feet should rest on the floor. 
What are some daily habits that reduce the impact of prolonged laptop use? (e.g., movement breaks, posture checks, eye strain reduction techniques)
Taking short breaks, every 60 mins can help reduce fatigue and regular posture checks can help.
